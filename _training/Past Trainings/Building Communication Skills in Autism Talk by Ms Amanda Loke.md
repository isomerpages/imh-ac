<h2>Building Communication Skills in Autism</h2>

<p></p>
<p>Ms Amanda Loke is a Principal Speech-Language Therapist at the Department
of Child Development, KKH. She has 14 years of experience working with
children with diverse developmental needs in hospital and school settings.
She believes strongly in partnering with key stakeholders to support holistic
development and community participation for children.</p>
<p>In this talk, Ms Amanda shares about the building blocks of communication
and how autism can affect language development and social communication.
She also demonstrates practical communication strategies on supporting
individuals with autism through daily activities.</p>
