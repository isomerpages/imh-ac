<h2>Changing Bodies, Changing Needs Talk by Ms Fathin Aerlisa</h2>

<p>Ms Fathin Aerlisa is a senior clinical psychologist at the Neurobehavioural
Clinic here at the IMH Child Guidance Clinic. She supports neurodiverse
individuals and works closely with families, educators, and allied health
professionals to enhance well-being, independence, and quality of life.</p>
<p>In "Changing Bodies, Changing Needs", she will be covering how puberty
and sexuality affect individuals with autism spectrum disorder, the unique
challenges and vulnerabilities they may face, and strategies for fostering
healthy, respectful relationships. The talk covers clear, safe ways to
teach these topics, guidance on addressing behaviours of concern, and practical
tools to build confidence in providing support.</p>
